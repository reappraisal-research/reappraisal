Training Introduction

We will be asking you to use two different strategies to change your perception of a few videos.

Situation Rethinking is about changing how you interpret the events of a situation so you feel less negative.
e.g. You see a video of some people falling and injuring themselves, but you tell yourself that it was likely just a stunt and no one got hurt.
e.g. You see a video of someone berating another person, but you tell yourself they are only doing it because they care about the other person and want them to be safe.

When you see the label Situation Rethinking after watching a video, use this strategy to decrease your negative emotions.


Goal Rethinking is about realizing how a negative event might actually serve other goals.
e.g. You see a movie where someone is crying, but you tell yourself the video is helping you practice using empathy.
e.g. You see a video of a car crash, but you tell yourself you now know to avoid similar situations in your life.

When you see the label Goal Rethinking after watching a video, use this strategy to decrease your negative emotions.
