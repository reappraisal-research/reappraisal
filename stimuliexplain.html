Image Instructions

You will be shown a photo before each new video you watch. Some of these images have 2 correct interpretations and are labeled Ambiguous.

When you see an image labeled Ambiguous, focus on seeing one interpretation of the image then the other, then back to the first, and so on as much as possible before the page switches.

When you see an image labeled Unambiguous, simply look at it until the page switches.

Once you have finished reading this and you understand what an ambiguous image is and what to do when you see it, please press the "Next" button.
