Ambiguous Stimuli Description

You will be shown a photo before each new video you watch. Some of these images are ambiguous
meaning there are multiple correct ways to interpret them. When you see an ambiguous image please
focus on shifting your perception back and forth between the different interpretations of the image.
Ambiguous images will be labeled so you know to look for both interpretations.
Once you have finished reading this and understand what an ambiguous image is and what
to do when you see it please press the "next" button.
